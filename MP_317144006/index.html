With roots going back to the year 2000, Cambridge Edu has grown to be the largest private sector higher education network in Sri Lanka due to an extensive island-wide network of 40 branches which create opportunities for over 30,000 students each year. Initially starting with Computing, Cambridge Edu today is a diversified education provider in the fields of ICT & Computing, Business Management, Hospitality Management, Engineering, Personal & Professional Development, Language Training and Corporate Training.
